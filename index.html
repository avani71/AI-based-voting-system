About the System

An AI-Based Voting System combines blockchain and artificial intelligence to create a secure, transparent, and fraud-resistant voting platform. It enhances traditional voting methods by ensuring immutability, anonymity, and trust in the voting process.

Key Features

✅ Blockchain Security – Ensures votes are tamper-proof and publicly verifiable.

✅ AI-Powered Insights – AI helps detect anomalies, prevent fraud, and optimize voter participation.

✅ Smart Contracts – Automate the voting process without intermediaries.

✅ Transparency & Privacy – Votes are immutable while keeping voter identities private.

✅ Remote Voting – Users can vote from anywhere using a web-based interface and blockchain wallet.
